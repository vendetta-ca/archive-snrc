Summary

An acoustic signal was recorded by the Arctic Division passive array during routine monitoring operations in the Fram Strait. The signal occurred once. Its origin has not been confirmed. Classification is Grade 3 due to disagreement between Arctic Division researchers and the Classification Review Board regarding the signal's source and significance. All personnel are accounted for. Observation is ongoing.

Signal description

The signal was recorded at 14:22 UTC on 2031-04-11. Duration was approximately 18 seconds. The waveform does not match catalogued ice noise, biological, or anthropogenic sources in this stratum. The Arctic Division monitoring team has noted internal structure within the signal. The Classification Review Board has assessed the signal as likely attributable to ice fracture events, which can produce complex waveforms in this region. This assessment is contested by the filing researcher.

The signal has not recurred as of the date of this record. Monitoring continues.
